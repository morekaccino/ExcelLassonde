Who we are

Excel Lassonde is a free tutoring service open to all majors, located at Lassonde. We offer help with many EECS, MATH, PHYS, and some CHEM courses.

Our goal is to make Lassondians’ academic journey easier by providing free tutoring with a multitude of courses, and as a result, reduce the drop out rate in first and second year at Lassonde.
